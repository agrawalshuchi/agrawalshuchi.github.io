Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Singapore Math?

A: Singapore Math is an approach that emphasizes problem-solving and understanding concepts deeply before moving on to more complex topics. It’s known for helping students build a strong foundation in math.

Q: How is the sliding scale fee determined?

A: The sliding scale allows families to pay what they can afford within the $25 to $40 range per class.

Q: What if my child needs additional help outside of class hours?

A: I offer additional one-on-one tutoring sessions if needed. These can be scheduled separately based on availability.
